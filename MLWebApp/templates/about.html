About

Smart Movie Recommendations is a movie recommender powered by a neural network recommendation engine.
Our engine takes user input genre preferences and selects movies that will fit those preferences.
Movies and movie data used to train the neural network came from the MovieLens dataset.

Look through all recommendations that have been generated for all users.
Or, create an account and generate your own recommendations which will be saved to your profile.
